Overview

Established in 1962 as MELECTRA Engineers, our company began with a team of five, focusing on sales and after-sales services for instruments from German and European suppliers. By 1967, we became the exclusive agent for Hartmann & Braun AG, Germany, and expanded our range of represented companies in the field of measuring and control.
In 1992, under the new name HAK Measurement & Automation, the two sons of founder Mr. Aly Radwan joined the company. Later, the company evolved into a Joint Stock Company, becoming HAK Automation S.A.E. under the leadership of Chairman Eng. Hazem Radwan.
For over 40 years, we have been market leaders in Gas Analysis, representing major manufacturers like Hartmann & Braun (now ABB) and Siemens. In 1996, we signed a representation agreement with a leading German company, and over the last 20 years, we've led the Emission Monitoring field with numerous CEMS installations, including Gas Analyzers, Dust Monitors, and Flow Monitors for industrial emissions.
HAK Automation is now a leading solutions provider in Process Analytics, Gas and Liquid Analysis, Environmental and Emission Monitoring, and Flow and Process Instrumentation. As a specialized system integrator and service provider, we serve industries such as Cement, Metal & Steel, Oil & Gas, Power, Fertilizer, and Chemicals & Petrochemicals.
