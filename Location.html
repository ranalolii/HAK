Location & Market Coverage

    Egypt is located in the heart of the world, it has a unique geographical place in the heart of Middle East and Africa. It is the point of convergence of the three old continents Africa, Asia and Europe. It also overlooks the Mediterranean and Red Sea along with the two Gulfs of Suez and Aqaba. On its lands the Suez Canal, one of the most important international waterways, is running.

    This strategic location allows us to serve a diverse range of markets and countries, from Egypt to Iraq, and as far South as Zambia and West of Nigeria. HAK Automation has leveraged Egypt's central position to not only deliver products, but also provide valuable expertise and competence to customers throughout the region.
