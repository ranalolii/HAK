Kiln Inlet + CEMS at Cemex Assiut Cement

Overview of the Cement Industry in Egypt

Egypt currently hosts 24 cement plants, with major players including Cemex, Lafarge, Italcementi, and Titan Cement. Out of these 24 plants, 16 are already equipped with gas analyzers, dust, and flow monitors from HAK Automation. The market for emission and process analyzers, especially Continuous Emission Monitoring Systems (CEMS), is experiencing significant growth in Egypt. This growth is largely driven by the 2011 amendment to the environmental law, which mandates all industries, particularly cement plants, to install CEMS solutions for gas and dust load calculations, as well as gas flow measurements.

Market potential for HAK

The local market potential for HAK Automation is considerable. This is attributed to two key factors:

  1. Advanced Technologies: HAK offers a variety of technologies and applications for process and emission measurement.
  2. Strong Local Support: Over the years, HAK has provided robust local support and service.

The HAK and Cemex Assiut Cement Plant Partnership

The collaboration between HAK and the Cemex Assiut cement plant began in 1999. A year after HAK signed a partnership agreement, HAK received its first order from the Assiut cement plant to supply nine dust monitors. This was followed by several more orders:

In 2013, the success story continued. HAK won a new project for the installation of two gas analyzer systems for two kiln inlet applications at the Assiut cement plant.

  1. 2006: Six new dust monitors were supplied, replacing BHA units, and the existing seven dust monitors were upgraded.
  2. 2008: HAK won its first installation of three units using hot-wet technology for raw mill stacks.

Project details and success factors

The recent kiln project exemplifies the strong collaboration between HAK and Cemex. HAK’s solutions were preferred due to:

  • Strong After-Sales Service: HAK’s proven local support and professional installation of existing CEMS solutions and dust monitors were crucial factors in Cemex’s evaluation.
  • Advanced Technology: HAK's hot-wet technology was chosen over competing solutions.
